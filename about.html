VISION

Empowering every pregnant woman with evidence-based information and confidence,transforming childbirth experiences for every family

MISSION

Our mission is to provide comprehensive Lamaze childbirth classes that empower pregnant women with evidence-based knowledge, practical skills, and emotional support. We strive to create a safe and nurturing environment where expectant parents can gain confidence, make informed decisions, and approach childbirth with positivity and empowerment. Through our commitment to excellence and compassionate care, we aim to make a lasting difference in the lives of every pregnant family we serve.

ABOUT THE EDUCATOR

Arthi Manikandan is an Internationally certified LAMAZE childbirth educator with 3 plus years experience in the New York city area. She has her Bachelor of engineering and MBA from prestigious universities in India. Her journey in first pregnancy introduced her to LAMAZE. She then felt this knowledge should reach every pregnant woman. She is passionate about educating and empowering pregnant couples in their journey from pregnancy to parenthood.
